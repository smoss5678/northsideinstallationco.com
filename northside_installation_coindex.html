About Northside

14 years in the trade.
Built into a specialty.

Steve Moss started out doing general trade work across plumbing, electrical, and carpentry. Over time the work narrowed. Kitchens and bathrooms became the focus, and appliance and fixture installation became the specialty.

Along the way that included high-volume installation work for Lowe's, Home Depot, Sears, Hawthorne Appliance, ABC Warehouse, and Sergeant Appliance. That kind of volume builds consistency and real problem-solving experience. The same expertise has also been trusted in some of the most exclusive homes in Southeast Michigan.

Steve is also an Authorized Factory Serviceman for Sub-Zero and Wolf, which is one of the harder credentials to hold in this trade. Northside Installation Co. is not a franchise or a dispatch crew. It is one person who has done this long enough to handle whatever a job throws at him.

"I started doing general trade work. I became a specialist. There is a real difference between the two, and after 14 years the clients can feel it."
