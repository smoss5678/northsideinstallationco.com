What We Do

Every service.
One specialist.

We handle the full scope from the appliance itself to the utility and site work that makes it function properly. One contractor, fewer headaches.

01
Appliance Installation

All major appliances installed correctly. Refrigerators, ranges, dishwashers, wall ovens, washers, dryers, and everything in between. Every brand, every model.

02 AFS Certified
Sub-Zero and Wolf Specialist

Authorized Factory Serviceman status isn't a marketing badge — it's a certification Sub-Zero and Wolf issue directly to technicians who meet their standards. It means installations are done to factory spec, and it's what the manufacturer requires for warranty work to remain valid. If your installer isn't AFS certified, your warranty may not be either.

03
Utility and Site Preparation

Gas connections, electrical upgrades, plumbing hookups, venting, cabinetry adjustments, custom cutouts, and structural prep. Everything the job needs before and after the appliance goes in.

04
New Construction

Subcontractor service from rough-in to final install. We show up on schedule, work clean, and coordinate with your timeline without creating problems for the rest of your crew.

05
Appliance Replacement

Old unit removal, disposal, and new installation handled in one visit. We coordinate around delivery schedules and handle the site work so you are not juggling multiple contractors.

06
Home Modification

Cabinetry cuts, floor protection, doorway clearance, and the structural work that makes an installation actually fit. We handle the surrounding work so the job is complete, not just started.

About Northside

14 years in the trade.
Built into a specialty.

Steve Moss started out doing general trade work across plumbing, electrical, and carpentry. Over time the work narrowed to what he was best at. Kitchens and bathrooms became the focus. Appliance and fixture installation became the specialty. That narrowing is intentional — it's what makes the difference between someone who can do the job and someone who has done it hundreds of times before.

The high-volume work for Lowe's, Home Depot, Sears, Hawthorne Appliance, ABC Warehouse, and Sergeant Appliance built the kind of consistency you only get from repetition at scale. The high-end work — in some of the most exclusive homes in Southeast Michigan — built the judgment to handle jobs where getting it wrong is not an option.

Northside Installation Co. is not a franchise, a dispatch service, or a crew of rotating technicians. It is one person who has been doing this long enough to have seen every way a job can go sideways, and who will answer the phone if it does.

"I started doing general trade work. I became a specialist. There is a real difference between the two, and after 14 years the clients can feel it."
